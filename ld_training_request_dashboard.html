Executive insights

01
Communication & AI top the demand chart. Together they account for ~42% of all requests and span every level — making them the highest-priority candidates for standardized, scalable programs.
02
Frontline staff (LT/L1/L2) drive volume. The highest number of training requests target junior levels, signaling a strong onboarding & foundational skills gap that can be addressed with structured induction programs.
03
Leadership pipeline needs attention. First-time manager transition, executive presence, coaching, and accountability appeared across 6+ teams — indicating a systemic mid-to-senior leadership readiness gap.
04
SW_A & GIC_Others_A are the highest-volume requestors. With 30 and 20 requests respectively, they warrant dedicated L&D account management, structured quarterly planning, and pre-approved training calendars.
05
12+ duplicate or overlapping requests exist across PnLs. Batching cross-team cohorts for common themes could reduce L&D delivery effort by an estimated 30–40% without compromising coverage.
